Automated Email Scheduler

The Automated Email Scheduler allows users to create and send emails on a scheduled basis using predefined templates and user-specific variables.

Key Features

  • Template-driven email generation
  • Custom variable substitution (e.g., #name, #date)
  • Scheduled sending via background workers
  • SMTP integration and delivery logging

This module is often paired with our Positive Pay system to send reports to banks and customers.
